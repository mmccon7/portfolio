ABOUT ME

I am an Arizona native of 20 plus years and enjoy living in the desert with my wife and 3 beautiful children. I currently work as a IT Support Manager / Sales Manager at a building supply company and spend my days checking website sales, taking phone orders, and troubleshooting computer user issues. I am super excited to delve into new web technologies and love a good challenge. No task is too great. I will find a way to make it happen and make sure to get it done on time...That's my motto.
I became interested in coding and website creation shortly after making a career/life change and foregoing the decision to pursue medical school after graduating college. I re-discovered my love for technology and decided to pursue that passion. I wanted to pursue Web Development because I love the idea that I can easily create an online presence or application from thin air. The sky's the limit. I am very artistic and love design, so naturally that part of Web Development snatched my attention from the get-go. Most importantly, I wanted to start a career in something I could be proud of, not just land another job, but achieve something greater.
